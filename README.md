 Key Insights from the Dashboard  
1. Outlet Performance: 
   - Supermarket Type 1 outlets contribute the majority of total sales.  
   - Smaller outlet types show potential for growth in targeted local markets.  

2. Product Category Trends:  
   - Fruits, Snacks, and Household items are top-selling product groups.  
   - Categories with lower average ratings correspond to lower sales volume.  

3. Customer Preferences:
   - Items with higher visibility and better ratings perform significantly better.  
   - Seasonal items drive short-term spikes in revenue.  

4. Sales Distribution:
   - Urban locations outperform rural ones due to higher customer footfall.  
   - Consistent sales patterns observed in older outlets, indicating brand loyalty.  

5. Strategic Recommendations: 
   - Focus marketing campaigns on high-rated, high-visibility items.  
   - Optimize stock levels in low-performing outlet types based on local demand.  
   - Implement targeted training for underperforming outlets to standardize customer experience.
